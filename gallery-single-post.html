MINDFREAK

PRIM AND PROPER

Mindfreak, whose real name is Aaron Leonhart, is a professional Valorant player from Indonesia who plays for Paper Rex, one of the top teams in the Asia-Pacific region. Known for his versatility and calm under pressure, Mindfreak excels as a controller player, often using agents like Astra, Viper, and Omen to anchor sites and support his team's strategy. His ability to think strategically and perform well in clutch moments has been crucial to Paper Rex's success in international competitions. Mindfreak's playstyle has earned him recognition as a key player in the competitive Valorant scene.
